About Me

I am Nawaraj Parajuli, currently serving as an Assistant Professor of Geology at Prithivi Narayan Campus, Tribhuvan University. This website serves as a hub for my teaching and research endeavors. You can easily explore different sections using the navigation tabs above. My expertise lies in the fields of structural and engineering geology. I completed my M.Sc. degree at Tribhuvan University and have been dedicatedly sharing my knowledge and passion for geology with students at Prithvi Narayan Campus since the year 2020.
